Jeff Bezos - $191 .7 billion

Amazon founder and CEO Jeff Bezos has a net worth of $191.7 billion and ranks as the second richest person on the earth today.
His position remains the same even after divorcing his wife MacKenzie in 2019 and transferring a quarter of his stake in Amazon to her.
Bezos founded the behemoth Amazon in 1994 out of his garage in Seattle. The e-commerce giant has reaped the benefits of this coronavirus pandemic,
with more people shopping online.
