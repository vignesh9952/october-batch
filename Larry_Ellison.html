Larry Ellison - $123.1 billion

Larry Ellison has a net worth of $123.1 billion which he earned from Oracle, the software firm he cofounded in 1977.
He quit as the CEO of the company in 2014 and since then he functions as the chairman of the board and chief technology advisor of the company.
Ellison has also been on the board of Tesla since December 2018 after he purchased 3 million shares earlier that year.
He owns nearly all of Hawaiian island Lanai.
